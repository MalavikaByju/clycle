Buy, Sell & Rent on Campus

Clycle is your secure campus marketplace where students can trade affordable campus essentials, rent items, and join the campus circular economy.

Campus-Only

Trade safely with verified students from your own university.

Sustainable

Reduce waste through repair, reuse, and peer-to-peer exchange.

Affordable

Find secondhand textbooks, clothing swaps, and budget-friendly essentials.

What We Built

  • Quick Posting — snap a pic, write two lines, choose sell/rent/donate.
  • Smart Search — filter textbooks by course code, clothes by actual size, electronics by working condition.
  • Campus Verified Chat — built-in messaging with university email verification.
  • Useful Filters — price range, condition, and location.
  • Wishlist System — save items and get alerts for similar listings.
  • Repair Resources — repair guides, local fix-it spots, and skill-sharing.

About Clycle

We are seven college students who got tired of seeing perfectly good stuff get thrown away while broke students like us desperately needed those exact items. Clycle is our attempt at fixing this ridiculous situation through a marketplace that actually gets student life.

"Look, we're just trying to make campus life less wasteful and more affordable. If that makes us idealists, then whatever, at least we're broke idealists with a decent app."

Our SEO Philosophy

We learned that building a cool app means nothing if people can't find it. Our strategy is simple: show up when students are actually searching for what we offer, be genuinely helpful, and build trust over traffic.

We focus on keywords like student marketplace, campus buy and sell platform, student-to-student trading, and campus sustainable marketplace because these represent real problems we're solving.

Our content plan includes blog posts, category pages, and repair hub guides — each targeting search intent stages from awareness to conversion. By creating helpful content and linking it to our platform, we build topical authority and trust with both students and search engines.

Design Philosophy

Our design philosophy for Clycle emphasizes simplicity, accessibility, and sustainability. We intentionally kept the interface minimal, using earthy tones and green accents to reflect our eco-conscious theme. Every element is designed to be intuitive, easy to navigate, and highly functional, prioritizing practical usability over flashy UI/UX. This approach was used to ensure that students can interact with the platform effortlessly while focusing on the core purpose of reusing, exchanging, and recycling items.

We are proud to highlight that Clycle is 93% more carbon-friendly than typical websites globally, earning an A rating on the Website Carbon Calculator: a testament to our commitment to a sustainable and responsible digital experience.

Philosophy of Logo

The Clycle logo symbolizes connection, continuity, and sustainability, reiterating the core ethos of our platform. Its interwoven, looping design represents the circular economy (the infinity) is a symbol to empower students to reuse, recycle, and exchange items within a supportive campus community. The overall logo symbolizes nurturing sustainable habits that grow organically just like a flourishing tree.
